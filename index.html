Konstantinos I. Stouras

Welcome to my page!

I am a Post-doctoral Researcher at the Darden School, University of Virginia and a Batten Institute Research Fellow. I study problems in innovation and services under the lens of Operations Management. I received a Ph.D. in Operations Management from INSEAD and hold graduate degrees in Financial Engineering, and Applied Mathematics and Physics from the National Technical University of Athens.
