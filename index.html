Post-doctoral Researcher in Operations Management




Welcome to my page!

I am a Post-doctoral Research Fellow at the Darden School, University of Virginia. I study problems in innovation and services under the lens of Operations Management. I received my Ph.D. in Operations Management from INSEAD, where I had the privilege to be advised by Serguei Netessine and Karan Girotra. I obtained a M.Sc. in Financial Engineering and a 5-year Diploma (M.Sc. equivalent) in Applied Mathematics and Physics at the National Technical University of Athens (ΕΜΠ), Greece.
