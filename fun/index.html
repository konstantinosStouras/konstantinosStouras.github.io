Fun Projects

A small collection of games and interactive tools I have been building for fun. Pick one and dive in.

  • The Lit — Research Paper Browser New

    Search papers across Management Science, Operations Research, Marketing Science, M&SOM, POM, Information Systems Research, five PNAS sections, the ACM EC conference (1999–present) and all 50 FT50 journals — in one place. Filter by journal type (FT50, ABS 4/4*, ABS 3), journal, year, author or affiliation, with editors and areas for Management Science, arXiv/SSRN PDF links for EC papers, and BibTeX export.

  • PortfolioFit New

    A strategic project-portfolio packing game: drag project “bricks”, each worth a dollar value, into a frame to maximise net value (minus a $1 penalty per empty cell) before the timer runs out — a hands-on take on the knapsack problem. The tempting high-ROI bricks are often traps.

  • Capitals New

    Learn the capital of every country in the world, one at a time, with graduated hints, a fact card and a world map locating each country after you answer. Plays in English or Greek, with progress, streaks and a leaderboard saved under your nickname.

  • Οι Νομοί της Ελλάδας Νέο · στα Ελληνικά

    Learn all 51 historical prefectures (νομοί) of Greece and their capitals on an interactive map, then test yourself with two click-the-map quizzes: find a prefecture, and find a prefecture by its capital.

  • Rook Battle

    Three two-player strategy puzzles of perfect information — Rook Battle, Queen Battle and Rook Battle x2. Outwit the optimal AI on a grid. Plays on mobile, tablet and desktop.

  • Sudoku

    A clean, modern Sudoku with Easy, Medium and Hard puzzles, each with a unique solution. Light or dark theme, English or Greek, per-number remaining counts, notes, hints, pause, a timer, and your game saved automatically.

  • Snake

    A modern take on Snake: one snake, many fruits. Eat to grow longer and faster, steering with the mouse or the arrow keys.

  • Management Science — Paper Browser

    Browse and filter every Management Science (INFORMS) article from 1954 to today. Search by accepting editor, area, year, author or affiliation, and export BibTeX citations. Optional sign-in lets you star papers and keep private notes, lists and tags.
