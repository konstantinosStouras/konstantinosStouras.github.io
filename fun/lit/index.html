Select a filter to get started

Pick a journal type (FT50, ABS 4/4*, ABS 3) or one or more journals, filter by year, or search by title, author, or affiliation. Everything is searched when no journal is selected (FT50-only journal files download on demand — a first all-journal search fetches the full dataset, so give it a moment).

Built as a personal project to search papers across several journals of my interest in one place.
Editors and editorial areas are shown for Management Science papers (2011+), exactly as on the MS-only browser. Other sources contribute their published papers and Articles in Advance. ACM EC papers link to an arXiv/SSRN/open-access PDF where one could be found.
The Journal types filter covers the full FT50 research rank — journals beyond the eight core sources are merged in from the FT50 browser's dataset, downloaded on demand. ABS ratings follow the Chartered ABS Academic Journal Guide (AJG 2024), as mirrored at journalranking.org; HBR and MIT Sloan Management Review, the AJG's "top practitioner" journals, are listed under ABS 3, their last numeric grade.
All metadata are publicly available, collected via the Crossref API (with OpenAlex, DBLP and Semantic Scholar assisting for EC PDF links). PNAS section labels come from pnas.org's own topic index where available, and are otherwise approximated from each paper's OpenAlex primary topic.
Feedback and suggestions are welcome. Any errors are my own. Konstantinos I. Stouras
