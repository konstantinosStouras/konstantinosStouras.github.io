How this works — click to expand
  1. Create a session (left): give it a name + code, choose the phases (which conditions to include and their order) and how many rounds participants play, and optionally reword any page. Hover the ? on any field for help.
  2. Save. You'll get a participant link (?code=…) and a ▶ Test link that lets you play it yourself (intro skipped, no data saved).
  3. Share the appropriate link with participants (via Prolific). Their data is tagged with this session's code.
  4. Watch progress under Active sessions (right); mark completed when done. See results on the Data and Analytics tabs.

The game: each participant searches a hidden line of 100 prizes, paying 5¢ to reveal any position, and keeps the best prize minus reveal costs. In the With AI phase they also get a free AI assistant that gives an estimate for any position — accurate near the positions it was trained on and unreliable elsewhere, but it always answers (participants aren't told where it is reliable). In the Without AI phase they search alone.

Ground truth is fixed. Every participant — in this and every session — sees the same hidden Brownian prize curves, so results are directly comparable. The Without AI and With AI phases use different curves, and each round within a phase is an independent draw (10 rounds ⇒ 10 different curves). Change TRUTH_SEED in config.js to reshuffle every curve at once.
