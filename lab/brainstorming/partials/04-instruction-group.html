Group Ideation Phase

Instructions

Welcome to the Group Phase of the challenge.

In this part, you’ll work together with your group to share, discuss, and refine ideas for new products in the health and wellness market. Each group member brings unique perspectives — use discussion and collaboration to strengthen your concepts.

You can build upon ideas from your teammates, combine different suggestions, or develop entirely new ones as a team. Focus on creating ideas that are clear, feasible, and innovative.

You’ll have a limited amount of time to complete this phase, so communicate efficiently and make sure every voice is heard.

Your task

  • Share and discuss individual ideas
  • Combine or improve them collaboratively
  • Record the best group-generated ideas
Work as one team — great ideas grow through collaboration!
