Welcome to the Ideation Challenge

An interactive study on creativity and decision-making

About the Experiment

Thank you for taking part in this study on idea generation and evaluation within the health and wellness domain. The task is designed to explore how individuals and groups create and select innovative product ideas.

  • Duration: Approximately 20–30 minutes in total.
  • Structure: You will go through individual ideation, group ideation, and a brief post-survey.
  • Anonymity: All responses are confidential and analyzed in aggregate form.
  • Requirements: Please complete the study in one sitting and avoid refreshing the page mid-session.

By proceeding, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old and consent to participate in this research.

You can exit the study at any time by closing your browser window.
